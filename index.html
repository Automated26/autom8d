How We Work

From idea to live in weeks

01

Discovery

We map your operations, identify the highest-ROI automation opportunities, and define success metrics. Usually done in a single 90-minute session.

02

Design

We architect the agent system, design the workflows, and plan integrations with your existing stack. You see and approve everything before a line of code is written.

03

Build & Train

We build, train, and test your agents on real scenarios. Typical delivery is 2–4 weeks depending on complexity. You get a staging environment to validate.

We go live, monitor performance, and continuously improve based on real-world data. Your agents get smarter over time — automatically.
